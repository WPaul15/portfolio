General Education Reflection

General education courses may seem like they aren't relevant to our studies. They're often seen as things we have to "get out of the way" before we get to our "real work". But these courses can teach us a lot, whether we realize it or not. This essay reflects upon my general education courses and how they affected my overall engineering studies and my viewpoints on problem solving.
